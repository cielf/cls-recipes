Orange Balls

(makes 3 doz)

Not exactly as shown

Description

These are a once a year treat in the Fisher household. One of those mid-century cuisine shouldn't be so good guilty pleasures. Orange and vanilla in icing sugar

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg (7.5 oz) vanilla wafers, crushed.
  • 3/4 cup icing sugar (note: the official recipe says confectioner's sugar. That's not easy to get here.)
  • 3/4 cup fine grated coconut
  • 1/2 cup frozen orange juice concentrate

Steps

  1. Mix crushed vanilla wafers, coconut and sugar.
  2. Add juice, mix well.
  3. form into 1" balls
  4. Roll in *additional* icing sugar
  5. Refrigerate overnight
