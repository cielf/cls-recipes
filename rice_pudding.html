Rice Pudding

Not exactly as shown

Description

This rice pudding is rice cooked in a lot of milk that reduces to a yummy creamy goodness. Raisins are optional - some like so many raisins that it should be called raisin pudding, and some prefer to leave the raisins out entirely

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Minute Rice. Note, you can use regular rice, but you need to add an extra cup of milk
  • 5 cups milk
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1-1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1-1/2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 egg yolk
  • raisins. I like a cup, but YMMV (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a large saucepan -- you'll probably want your largest pan with a stick handle
  2. Bring to a boil -- I would do this over high-medium heat. Watch it carefully, because milk boils over very easily
  3. Cook over medium heat for 30 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes
  4. Remove from heat.
  5. Beat an egg yolk slightly, and add a wee bit of the hot rice mixture to it and mix well. Mix in the raisins if using
  6. Return the eggy mixture to the saucepan
  7. Heat to boiling
  8. Remove from heat
  9. Serve warm or chilled. It is very nice warm with a bit of heavy cream
